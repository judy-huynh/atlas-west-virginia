West Virginia County Risk Profile

Every county, every risk, in one place. All 55 counties, with disaster, funding, vulnerability, and flood data. Click any column to sort. Use the compound risk explorer to find the counties carrying several risks at once.

Source. data/wv_county_profile.csv. Disaster counts and FEMA totals reconcile exactly to the Atlas of Accountability 2011–2024 workbook on all 55 counties. See About the data for every column and where it comes from.
Limitations. Buyout years are missing for 45% of properties. Data centers, coal plants, mining, and landownership are not in this file.
